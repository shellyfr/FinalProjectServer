Instructions:



During the task you will be presented with a series of web pages.
On the first page you will be asked to rate your level of agreement of various issues.
Please be honest. Afterwards you will be presented with information about a particular topic and
you will be asked to reply to questions about it.

To complete the task you must complete all pages until you reach the final page.
It is not possible to move backwards between the pages, so move forward only when you
are completely satisfied with the answers you have provided.
The task should be performed alone and independently.
You are requested not to pass on information about the content of the task to any other
participant.

To get the payout you must complete the task and answer all the questions.
Please note that the task must be performed only once! Performing the task a number of
times will be rewarded for a single execution.
